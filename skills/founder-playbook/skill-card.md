# Skill Card

## Description

founder-playbook provides decision validation and structured thinking frameworks (GROW, Solution-Focused, Socratic questioning) for startup founders pressure-testing decisions and next steps. It combines coaching methodology with 2025 startup knowledge covering fundraising, customer development, runway management, and crypto/web3 specifics.

This skill is ready for commercial and non-commercial use.

## Use Case

Startup founders (including crypto/web3 founders) who want a structured thinking partner to sanity-check a decision, work through strategic options, surface blind spots, and commit to concrete next steps - not a source of legal, financial, or medical advice.

## Known Risks and Mitigations

Risk: Ecosystem data is time-stamped (last updated November 2025) - grant amounts, accelerator terms, survey statistics, and regulatory notes may be stale when used.

Mitigation: The skill states its data date up front; founders should verify concrete figures (grants, programs, regulations) against current primary sources before acting.

Risk: Founders may treat coaching output as professional advice for high-stakes fundraising, legal, or financial decisions.

Mitigation: The skill's methodology is autonomy-supportive - it asks questions rather than prescribing - and explicitly signals when switching to direct advice; consult qualified professionals for legal/financial matters.

Risk: The skill touches founder mental health topics and could drift into quasi-therapeutic territory.

Mitigation: It includes explicit red-flag criteria and instructs redirecting to professional support (therapy, crisis resources) rather than diagnosing, and names "scope creeper" drift as an anti-pattern to avoid.
