## Known Risks and Mitigations

Risk: The skill covers token launches (launchpads, LP management, timing), an area with significant financial and legal exposure; treating its tactical guidance as compliance advice could lead to regulatory violations.

Mitigation: The skill explicitly conditions token launches on jurisdiction-specific legal review and discourages launching without proven utility; users must obtain their own legal counsel before any token event.

Risk: Market data in the skill (launchpad market shares, grant program amounts, platform policies such as the January 2026 X crackdown) is a snapshot of the February 2026 landscape and decays quickly.

Mitigation: The skill states its landscape date up front; verify current figures, program availability, and platform policies before acting on any number.

Risk: Growth tactics (KOL engagement, airdrop design) can shade into undisclosed promotion or sybil-exploitable incentives if misapplied.

Mitigation: The skill's own anti-patterns section forbids undisclosed paid shilling and airdrop-farming incentives; follow the disclosure requirements it documents.
